How to play
Phase 1 — Roll & Action card: Roll 2 dice and move your token along the winding road. Draw an action card — read it aloud and discuss with everyone before the explanation reveals.
Phase 2 — Question card: Answer a diabetes question. A full clinical explanation always appears after your answer — right or wrong.
Phase 3 — Gift draw: Answer correctly and flip a mystery gift card to reveal your points. Fortune cards add surprise twists!
The player with the most points when someone reaches Home wins!
Knowledge card rule
When an action card is drawn: (1) Player reads it aloud to everyone. (2) Player explains in their own words. (3) Others add or correct. (4) Facilitator or guidebook gives the full explanation — already shown on screen.
