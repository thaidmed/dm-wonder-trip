How to play
Phase 1 — Roll & Action card: Roll 2 dice, watch your token walk tile by tile. Draw an action card — read aloud, discuss, then the explanation reveals.
Phase 2 — Question card: Answer a diabetes question. Full explanation always shows after — right or wrong.
Phase 3 — Gift draw: Answer correctly and flip a mystery gift card to reveal your points!
Most points when someone reaches Home wins!
Knowledge card rule
When an action card is drawn: (1) Player reads aloud. (2) Explains in own words. (3) Others add/correct. (4) Facilitator or guidebook explains fully — shown on screen.
